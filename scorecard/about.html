About the Maturity Scorecard Tool

The Maturity Scorecard Tool is a project developed by TechBridge Consulting (TBC) in collaboration with Medline as part of the i.c.stars Cycle 53 program. It aims to provide a centralized platform for auditing and measuring various aspects of Medline's processes against defined standards and best practices, with the ability for users to define custom scorecards and criteria for conducting audits.
