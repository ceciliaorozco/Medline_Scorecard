Privacy Notice (Prototype)

This is a prototype of the Maturity Scorecard Tool project by TechBridge Consulting (TBC) in collaboration with Medline. Please note the following:

Data Collection

This prototype does not collect or store any data externally. All data is processed locally on your device for demonstration purposes only. No user information is transmitted over the internet or stored on remote servers.

About the Project

The Maturity Scorecard Tool is designed to help Medline improve its internal processes and practices. It is currently in development as part of the i.c.stars Cycle 53 program.
